# **AndyBot**

### A Chatbot Adventure across Pittsburgh's Museums
<ArticleTags tags={metadata.stack}/>

In 2018 I joined the Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh as lead developer for the Innovation Studio. Having secured a $150,000 [grant from the Knight Foundation](https://knightfoundation.org/articles/museums-technology-new-avenues-for-wonder/), our goal was to create a chatbot that museum guests could engage with inside and outside the museum. We partnered with Advancement and Community Engagement (ACE) team to digitize their existing Summer Adventure program and open it up for all our visitors. We [open sourced our code](https://github.com/CMP-Studio/andybot-botpress) and presented our findings at the [American Alliance of Museums conference in 2018](https://www.aam-us.org/2018/04/10/chat-with-andybot-at-aam-2018/). You can read more about CarnegieBot in this
[blog post](https://studio.carnegiemuseums.org/a-chatbot-adventure-c729812571ea).
